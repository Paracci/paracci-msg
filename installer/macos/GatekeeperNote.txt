Paracci for macOS is distributed without Apple notarization.

Because this project does not use a paid Apple Developer account, Gatekeeper may
block the first launch after you copy Paracci.app to Applications.

On macOS Ventura and later:
1. Try to open Paracci once.
2. Open System Settings > Privacy & Security.
3. Select Open Anyway for Paracci and confirm the prompt.

On older macOS versions:
1. Try to open Paracci once.
2. Open System Preferences > Security & Privacy > General.
3. Select Open Anyway for Paracci and confirm the prompt.

Only approve the app if you downloaded it from the official Paracci release.
